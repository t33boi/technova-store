Our Story

Bringing World-Class Tech to Nigeria

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Lagos, TechNova was born from a simple vision: to make premium technology accessible to every Nigerian. We saw a gap between the demand for quality gadgets and what was available locally, and we set out to bridge it.

Today, we are one of Nigeria's fastest-growing e-commerce platforms for IT gadgets, serving over 50,000 customers across all 36 states. From smartphones to laptops, audio gear to cameras, we curate only the best products from trusted global brands.
